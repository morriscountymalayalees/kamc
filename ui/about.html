About Us

We Connect Malayalees Around Morris County

We strive to make a home away from home and build a community.

Team KAMC, Founders

Kerala Association of Morris County is a non-profit initiative dedicated to celebrating and sharing the diverse cultural heritage of Kerala. Our mission is to educate, inspire, and connect people through the traditions, art forms, festivals, and culinary delights that make Kerala unique.
